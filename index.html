Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"


Innovation

Fostering a culture of creativity and technological advancement to drive economic growth and improve quality of life.


Sustainability

Committing to eco-friendly practices and renewable energy to ensure a balanced and resilient environment for future generations


Infrastructure

Developing robust transportation, communication, and energy systems to support economic activities and enhance connectivity.


Empowerment

Promoting education, equality, and opportunities to enable individuals to contribute meaningfully to society and the economy.
